# About

![Andreas Farre](/assets/images/profile.jpg){: .profile-photo}

get to poke at all sorts of DOM things, navigation, session history, and
whatever else comes up. I also spend some time working on specs for the stuff I
touch.

This blog is where I write about things I'm working on or find interesting.
